# Hollard Insurance Products Overview

## Life Insurance Products

### Life Cover
Life insurance provides financial protection for your loved ones when you pass away. Hollard offers comprehensive life cover that pays out a lump sum to your beneficiaries, helping them maintain their lifestyle and meet financial obligations.

**Key Benefits:**
- Lump sum payment to beneficiaries
- Cover for natural death and accidental death
- Optional additional benefits available
- Affordable premiums based on age and health
- Tax-free payout to beneficiaries

**Who Needs Life Cover?**
- Parents with dependent children
- Primary income earners in households
- People with outstanding debts (mortgages, loans)
- Business owners wanting to protect their families
- Anyone wanting to leave a financial legacy

### Disability Cover
Disability cover provides income replacement if you become unable to work due to illness or injury. This ensures you can continue to meet your financial obligations even when you cannot earn an income.

**Coverage Includes:**
- Monthly income replacement (typically 75% of gross salary)
- Cover for temporary and permanent disability
- Occupation-specific definitions
- Waiting period options (1, 3, or 6 months)
- Benefits paid until recovery or retirement age

**Types of Disability:**
- **Temporary Disability**: Short-term inability to work with expected recovery
- **Permanent Disability**: Long-term or permanent inability to work in your occupation
- **Total Disability**: Complete inability to perform any work

### Critical Illness Cover
Critical illness insurance pays a lump sum if you're diagnosed with a specified critical illness. This allows you to focus on recovery without financial stress.

**Covered Conditions:**
- Cancer (all major forms)
- Heart attack
- Stroke
- Kidney failure
- Major organ transplant
- Coronary artery bypass surgery
- Multiple sclerosis
- Parkinson's disease
- Loss of limbs
- Paralysis

**Benefits:**
- Lump sum payout on diagnosis
- Use funds for any purpose (treatment, debt, lifestyle)
- No requirement to stop working
- Can be claimed alongside other policies
- Covers multiple conditions in one policy
