# About Hollard Insurance

## Our Story

### History and Heritage
Hollard is South Africa's largest privately-owned insurance group, with a rich history spanning over 40 years. Founded with a vision to provide accessible, quality insurance to South Africans, Hollard has grown from humble beginnings to become a trusted name in the insurance industry.

**Timeline:**
- **1980s:** Hollard founded as a South African insurance company
- **1990s:** Expanded product offerings and broker network
- **2000s:** Regional expansion across Africa
- **2010s:** Digital transformation and innovation
- **2020s:** Focus on sustainability and Better Futures mission

### The Hollard Name
The name "Hollard" represents our Dutch heritage and our commitment to being a solid, dependable partner for our customers. We take pride in our independence as a privately-owned company, which allows us to make decisions that prioritize long-term customer relationships over short-term profits.

## What We Do

### Our Business Model
Hollard operates exclusively through a network of professional insurance brokers. We believe that insurance is not a commodity - it's a critical financial decision that benefits from expert, personalized advice. Our broker model ensures:

- Customers receive professional guidance
- Policies are tailored to individual needs
- Ongoing support throughout the policy lifetime
- Expert claims assistance
- Objective product comparisons
