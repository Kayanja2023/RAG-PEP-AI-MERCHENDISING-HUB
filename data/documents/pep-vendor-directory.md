## Vendor Onboarding Process

When considering a new supplier:

1. **Initial Contact:** Request company profile and references
2. **Factory Visit:** Assess facilities and quality systems
3. **Sample Evaluation:** Test quality, fit, finish (3-5 samples)
4. **Costing Analysis:** Compare landed cost vs. current suppliers
5. **Trial Order:** Small quantity test (typically 500-1,000 units)
6. **Performance Review:** After 3 deliveries, assess rating
7. **Preferred Status:** Achieved after 6 months consistent performance

**Required Documentation:**
- BBBEE Certificate
- Tax Clearance
- Company Registration
- Bank Details
- Insurance Certificates
- Quality Certifications (where applicable)

## Lead Time Planning Guide

**Critical Seasons & Order Timing:**

| Season | Category | Order By | Delivery Target |
|--------|----------|----------|----------------|
| Back-to-School | School uniforms, shoes | October | December-January |
| Autumn/Winter | Knitwear, jackets | August | March-April |
| Spring/Summer | Summer clothing, sandals | January | September-October |
| Festive | Gift items, party wear | June | November |

**Import Lead Times:**
- China: 90-120 days (sea freight)
- India: 75-90 days (sea freight)
- Air freight: Add 50-100% to cost, reduces time by 60-75 days
